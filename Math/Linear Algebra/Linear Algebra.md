
**Definition**
Linear algebra is a branch of mathematics that studies vectors, matrices, and linear transformations to solve n linear equations with n unknowns.

## Multiplication

#### Dot product

A dot product is carried out for two vectors or matrix rows/columns as
$$
a \cdot b = \sum^{n}_{i=1}{a_{i}b_{i}}
$$
Where a and b are two same-length elements of size $n$.
